SxS Shelter Planner

Single-bay equipment shelter — Can-Am Defender MAX HD11

Materials Estimate

Estimated quantities for the fixed 10 × 16 ft shelter. Low side 8 ft, high side 10'-6". Verify with your lumber yard before ordering.

Build Notes

Foundation & Leveling on Uneven Ground

  1. Lay out 6 post locations with string lines: 4 corners + 1 mid-span on each 16 ft side (8 ft on center).
  2. Dig each hole to frost line — 48 in for Catskills, NY. Adjust for your region.
  3. Drop 3-4 in of gravel in each hole for drainage. Set a ground-contact-rated PT 6×6 post in each hole, plumb, and pour concrete. Brace until cured.
  4. Each post lands at whatever ground elevation it sits at — uneven ground is absorbed by how much post sticks above grade.
  5. After concrete cures (24-48 hrs), snap two level lines: one for the low side (left) at 8 ft, one for the high side (right) at 10'-6", both referenced from the same datum.
  6. Cut the three low-side posts to the 8 ft line; cut the three high-side posts to the 10'-6" line. Both rows are level within themselves; the difference between rows creates the roof pitch.
  7. Set the double 2×8 PT beams on the cut tops on each side and fasten with structural screws or Simpson post caps.

Roof Details

  • 3/12 pitch runs across the 10 ft width — low side (left) 8 ft, high side (right) 10'-6". Rafters span the width; purlins run the 16 ft length.
  • 2×6 rafters at 24 in OC along the 16 ft depth — 9 rafters total. Each spans the 10 ft width from the low-side beam to the high-side beam (~10.31 ft along the slope).
  • Water drains off the high side (right). Keep that edge clear; a gutter here is a good upgrade.
  • Hurricane ties (Simpson H1 or equivalent) at every rafter-to-beam connection.
  • Low-side eave: 12 in overhang past the left-side posts. High side: flashing or closure strip against the high-side beam.
  • Foam closure strips at both the low eave edge and the high-side flashing seal corrugations against pests and wind-driven precip.

Defender MAX HD11 Clearance

  • Vehicle dimensions: 162.4 in L × 65 in W × 81.4 in H (13.5 ft × 5.4 ft × 6.8 ft). Wheelbase 117.5 in.
  • Bay width 10 ft (120 in) gives ~27 in total side clearance — enough to open both doors and walk around.
  • Bay depth 16 ft (192 in) gives ~30 in of front+rear clearance combined.
  • Low-side wall height 8 ft (96 in) clears the 81.4 in vehicle height by ~15 in — enough for a roof rack or light bar. High side clears by ~33 in.
  • A simple PT 2×6 wheel stop at ~14 ft from the front prevents the bumper from contacting the back wall.
